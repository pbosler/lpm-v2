Detailed Description

Each faces knows the indices (to Particles) of its vertices and center and the indices (to Edges) of its edges.

Designed to accomodate quadrilateral and triangular faces. Faces are recursively divided to provide desired spatial resolution, and stored in the induced quadtree.
