SWEPlaneSolver

Data structure for solving the Shallow Water Equations (SWE) in the beta plane. Used with the PlanarSWE module. More...

Detailed Description

Data structure for solving the Shallow Water Equations (SWE) in the beta plane. Used with the PlanarSWE module.

The shallow water equations are presented in the detailed description of the PlanarSWE module.
Integrals are used to compute the velocity \(\vec{u}\), the double dot product \( \nabla\vec{u}:\nabla\vec{u} \), and a PSE integral (see PSEDirectSum) approximates the Laplacian of the fluid surface.
As with the other integrals in LPM, these integrals are computed as a parallel direct summation across all MPI ranks.
See sweplanesolvermodule::sweplanerhsintegrals.

All other terms are computed as ODEs along each particle trajectory; see sweplanesolvermodule::timestepprivate.

Computes the integral expressions on the right-hand side of the set of ODEs from a Lagrangian method of lines discretization; see PlanarSWE.

Integrals are used to compute the double dot product \( \nabla\vec{u}:\nabla\vec{u} \) and a PSE integral (see PSEDirectSum) approximates the Laplacian of the fluid surface.
As with the other integrals in LPM, these integrals are computed as a parallel direct summation across all MPI ranks.

[out]vy-component of velocity
[out]doubleDotdouble dot product
[out]lapSurfLaplacian of fluid surface
[in]xx-coordinates of particles
[in]yy-coordinates of particles
[in]vortvorticity carried by particles
[in]divdivergence carried by particles
[in]hfluid depth at particles
[in]topoFnbottom topography height function, must conform to numberkindsmodule::scalarFnOf2DSpace interface
[in]areaarea represented by each particle
[in]maskmask(i) is true if particle i is active
[in]pseEpsPSE particle influence radius
[in]mpiParticlesMPISetup to distribute work of particle integration across all MPI ranks
