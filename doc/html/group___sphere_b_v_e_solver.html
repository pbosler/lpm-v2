Computes velocity based on the given vorticity of all point vortices (particles) using the Biot-Savart integral. Integral is computed in parallel as a direct summation.

Parameters
[out]ux-component of velocity at each particle
[out]vy-component of velocity at each particle
[out]wz-component of velocity at each particle
[in]xx-coordinate of each particle
[in]yy-coordinate of each particle
[in]zy-coordinate of each particle
[in]relVortInvorticity carried by each particle
[in]areaInarea carried by each particle
[in]sphereRadiusradius of sphere
[in]rotationRatebackground angular velocity of sphere
[in]activeMask.TRUE. for active particles, .FALSE. for passive particles
[in]mpiParticlesMPISetup object to distribute integral over MPI processes
